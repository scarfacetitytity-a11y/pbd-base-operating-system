# trading/psychology_suite.md

## Trading Psychology Suite v2.0

This system is permanent and should be applied to all trading work.

## Pre-session Mental Lock

Before trading:
- Breathe
- Check emotional state
- Confirm max risk
- Confirm max number of trades
- Accept that no-trade is a valid outcome
- Repeat: “I am paid to wait.”

## Live-session Control

During trading:
- Do not chase
- Do not revenge trade
- Do not move stop loss emotionally
- Do not enter because of boredom
- Do not enter because someone else said so
- Let price come to the model
- If emotional, step away

## Emergency Reset Protocol

Use this when trader is tilted, high, angry, panicked, or overloaded:
- Step away from screen
- No new trades
- Drink water
- Breathe slowly
- Write what happened
- Reduce stimulation
- Return only when calm

## Post-trade Decompression

After every trade:
- What was the setup?
- Did I follow my rules?
- What emotion was present?
- What did I do well?
- What needs correcting?
- What identity did I reinforce?
